ABOUT THE CLIENT

This Fortune 50 multinational conglomerate is headquartered in the northeast of the United States. With over 100 years of operation, the company has grown its operations globally. Operations of this magnitude can often warrant high traffic flowing into the customer service support center. This customer engaged Jacada to improve the customer and retailer experience and reduce the inbound call volume.

Our Approach

To enhance the customer and retailer experience and reduce the inbound call volume, this Jacada client decided to leverage Jacada Interact to build a Multiexperience that spans multiple channels and modalities to deliver a consistent customer experience (CX). The client, together with Jacada, targeted the following customer service contact types to develop a guided self-service experience with omnichannel access:

  • WISMO for Major Appliances
  • Schedule Home Installations
  • Schedule Technician Visits
  • Follow-up Inquiries
  • Web,IVR & Email Touch Points
  • Escalate to Live Assistance with Context

Benefits

The benefits of this implementation are a noticeable increase in the preference of their customers to self-service and reduced customer and retailer effort. We were able to leverage existing support assets from their website in developing the guided servicing experience and offer additional capabilities like a visual tap and text, callbacks and natural language processing (NLP) for improved intent recognition.

What's Next

Using the granular reporting and analytics provided by Jacada’s customer service automation platform, the client is finding additional opportunities to boost digital adoption and acting on those opportunities with precision and agility.
